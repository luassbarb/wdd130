HISTORY

We are a Portuguese company. We make waves and create memories since 2023. Rafting was discovered in 1869, when John Wesley Powel organized the first expedition on the Colorado River, USA, in boats with central oars. In the beginning, the adventurers didn't have any technique to maneuver rigid boats in the rapids, and they had problems with overturning and colliding with rocks. Our company's focus is to keep this sport active today.
